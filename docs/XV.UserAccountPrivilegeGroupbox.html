Class: UserAccountPrivilegeGroupbox

UserAccountPrivilegeGroupbox

new UserAccountPrivilegeGroupbox

Manages the assignment of privileges to user accounts. This is a complicated case because user accounts can have privileges directly assigned to them, but they can also have privileges available to them on account of a role that they're assigned to.
